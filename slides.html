Mikasa Ackerman

Background: Tragic past; saved by Eren Yeager; deeply bonded with him.

Skills and Abilities

Part of the Ackerman clan, granting superhuman strength and combat instincts. Top-tier fighter in the Survey Corps.

Personality

Fiercely loyal to Eren, stoic yet caring. Struggles with inner conflict and her place in the world.

Symbolism

The red scarf symbolizes comfort and protection. Represents freedom and family for Mikasa.

Trivia

Enjoys bread and meat. Inspired by strong female characters from other media.
